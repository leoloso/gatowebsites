Execute a single GraphQL query to **search and replace a string in all posts in your site**, even including regular expressions.

You will be able to replace the domain after a site migration, modify all image URLs after introducing a CDN, remove styles hardcoded in HTML code, replace the referenced image sizes, and much more.
