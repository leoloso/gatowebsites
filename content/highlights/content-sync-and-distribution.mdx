**Synchronize and distribute content across multiple WordPress sites**, whether a multisite or independent sites.

You can also fetch content from, and export content to, non-WordPress sites that offer an API.

_In the video: Content is copied to a staging site, from where it is reviewed by a 3rd-party editor, and then it is copied back to the production site._
