**Retrieve data from, and post data to, any external service** that offers an API.

Convenient methods for REST and GraphQL APIs are provided, making it easy to interact with Notion, AirTable, GitHub, Mailchimp, Zapier, OpenAI, ConvertKit, and many more.

In the video: A copy of the website users' emails is sent to AirTable.
